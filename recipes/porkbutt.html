Pork Butt

A delicious slow cooked pork dinner, best served with family and friends

This meal takes a little time to prepare, but a lot of time to cook. Required: patience and a smoker

Ingredients

  • Pork butt or shoulder (bone in for best results)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • or skip all of the above and use Hot BBQ rub

Additional items needed

  • Aluminum pan with foil
  • wood chunks (Any fruit wood works)

Steps

  1. Prepare smoker for indirect heat: 275F(135C).
  2. trim excess fat from Pork butt, down to 1/4"(.63cm)
  3. season all sides with salt, pepper, and garlic, or skip all of these and use your favorite Hot BBQ rub.
  4. Place Pork Butt on smoker, add wood chunks
  5. smoke for 4-5 hours maintaining consistent heat and smoke
  6. check internal temperature, and continue to smoke until reaches 160F(71C)
  7. Once reaches temperature, place Pork Butt in pan, seal with foil, & return to smoker
  8. continue cooking until internal temperature reaches 200F(93.3C)
  9. remove Pork Butt and let rest for at least 1 hour hour
    • Mainly let the juices reconstitute
  10. once ready, shred with tool or gloved hands.
  11. enjoy!
